I am a full-time research assistant at MBZUAI, Abu Dhabi, where I work on computer vision and machine learning under the supervision of Dr Fahad Shahbaz Khan. I mostly work on generative models and visual perception models from a small amount of data. Previously, I have worked with Dr Yang Wang at the University of Manitoba, Canada and Dr Partha Roy. at IIT Roorkee, India. I have done my bachalors from Jadavpur University, Kolkata in Electrical Engineering.
